# Game Events

Build a calendar of all the most important gaming events for you game. Attend to stay in touch with what's going on in the industry. Get involved when you have the time to prep and share your game, asset, or innovation.

* [E3](https://e3expo.com)
* [PAX](https://www.paxsite.com)
* [Game Connection](https://www.game-connection.com)
