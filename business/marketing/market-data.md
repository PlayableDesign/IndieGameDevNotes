# Market Data

Market data helps verify your ideas about games that are like yours. Use to help you narrow your target audience, platform, genre, etc. and to provide support in your pitch.

* [games-stats.com](https://games-stats.com)
* [newzoo](https://newzoo.com)
* [IGDB](https://www.igdb.com)
* [Gameopedia](https://www.gameopedia.com)
* [Switch Scores](https://www.switchscores.com)
* [Steam Spy](https://steamspy.com)
* [Steam Store](https://store.steampowered.com/stats/)
