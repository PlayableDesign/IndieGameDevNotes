# Game Reviews

Approach game review sites and reviewers to try your game. Just make sure it's ready! It would be wise to do this incrementally, start by asking your friends and family to play. Then expand to a larger group, then maybe one reviewer. As you gain confidence approach more reviewers.

## Game Review Sites

* [IGN](https://www.ign.com/reviews/games)
